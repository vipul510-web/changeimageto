# Keyword Research Guide for Programmatic SEO

## Quick Start

1. **Use the keyword expansion script** to generate variations
2. **Research search volumes** using Google Keyword Planner
3. **Add to CSV** with proper categorization
4. **Generate pages** using the scripts

## Keyword Categories

### Functionality Pages (Tool Pages)

#### 1. Platform-Specific Keywords
**Format**: `[tool] for [platform]`

**Platforms to target:**
- E-commerce: Shopify, Amazon, eBay, Etsy, WooCommerce, BigCommerce
- Social Media: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest, TikTok
- Marketplaces: Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, OfferUp
- Print: Print-on-demand (Printful, Printify), Zazzle, Redbubble

**Examples:**
- `remove background for shopify`
- `upscale image for amazon`
- `enhance image for etsy`
- `bulk resize images for shopify`

#### 2. Industry-Specific Keywords
**Format**: `[tool] for [industry]`

**Industries to target:**
- E-commerce, Real Estate, Fashion, Jewelry, Furniture, Food & Beverage
- Photography, Marketing, Advertising, Web Design
- Print, Publishing, Manufacturing

**Examples:**
- `remove background for ecommerce`
- `upscale image for real estate`
- `enhance image for fashion`
- `blur background for portraits`

#### 3. Use Case-Specific Keywords
**Format**: `[tool] for [use case]`

**Use Cases:**
- Product photos, Portraits, Logos, Social media posts
- Print, Web, Email marketing, Presentations
- LinkedIn photos, Dating app photos, Professional headshots

**Examples:**
- `remove background for product photos`
- `upscale image for print`
- `enhance image for web`
- `blur background for portraits`

#### 4. Color-Specific Keywords
**Format**: `change background to [color]`

**Colors to target:**
- Primary: Red, Green, Blue, Yellow, Orange, Purple
- Secondary: Pink, Gray, Navy, Teal, Brown, Black, White
- Brand colors: Specific hex codes for popular brands

**Examples:**
- `change background to red`
- `change background to navy`
- `change background to teal`

### Blog Pages (Content Pages)

#### 1. Platform Tutorials
**Format**: `how to [tool] for [platform]`

**Examples:**
- `how to remove background for shopify`
- `how to upscale image for amazon`
- `how to enhance image for etsy`

#### 2. Use Case Tutorials
**Format**: `how to [tool] for [use case]`

**Examples:**
- `how to remove background from product photo`
- `how to upscale image for print`
- `how to enhance image for web`

#### 3. Comparison Pages
**Format**: `best [tool] for [platform/use case]` or `[tool A] vs [tool B]`

**Examples:**
- `best background remover for shopify`
- `best image upscaler for print`
- `changeimageto vs remove bg`

#### 4. Problem-Solution Pages
**Format**: `[tool] without [constraint]` or `[tool] free [platform]`

**Examples:**
- `remove background free shopify`
- `upscale image without software`
- `enhance image free online`

2. **SEMrush** - Competitor analysis
3. **Moz Keyword Explorer** - Keyword difficulty

## Keyword Expansion Strategy

### Step 1: Seed Keywords
Start with your main tools:
- remove background
- upscale image
- enhance image
- blur background
- change background
- remove text
- remove people
- change color
- convert format
- bulk resize

### Step 2: Add Modifiers
For each tool, add:
- Platforms (Shopify, Amazon, Etsy, etc.)
- Industries (e-commerce, real estate, fashion, etc.)
- Use cases (product photos, portraits, print, etc.)
- Colors (red, green, blue, etc.)

### Step 3: Generate Variations
Use the expansion script or manually create:
- `[tool] for [platform]`
- `[tool] for [industry]`
- `[tool] for [use case]`
- `how to [tool] for [platform]`
- `best [tool] for [platform]`

### Step 4: Research & Filter
- Check search volumes (target 10-1000 monthly)
- Check competition (low to medium)
- Check user intent (transactional/informational)

## CSV Format

```csv
keyword,page_type,category,search_volume,difficulty,template_type,page_type_category,variables
remove background for shopify,functionality,use_case_specific,800,30,use_case_template,remove_background,"{""platform"": ""Shopify"", ""use_case"": ""e-commerce""}"
```

**Required Fields:**
- `keyword`: The target keyword
- `page_type`: `functionality` or `blog`
- `category`: Category for organization
- `search_volume`: Monthly search volume (0 if unknown)
- `difficulty`: Competition difficulty (0-100)
- `template_type`: Template to use
- `page_type_category`: Tool type (remove_background, upscale, etc.)
- `variables`: JSON with platform, use_case, industry, etc.

## Priority Order

### Phase 1: High-Value Keywords (Week 1-2)
- Platform-specific (Shopify, Amazon, Etsy)
- High search volume (500+)
- Low competition (< 30)

### Phase 2: Medium-Value Keywords (Week 3-4)
- Industry-specific (e-commerce, real estate)
- Medium search volume (100-500)
- Medium competition (30-50)

### Phase 3: Long-Tail Keywords (Week 5+)
- Use case-specific
- Low search volume (10-100)
- Low competition (< 30)
