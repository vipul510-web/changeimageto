    How to Resize Images for Facebook

    Our free online tool makes it easy to resize images for Facebook. Simply upload your images above, choose your Facebook format, and click resize. No signup required, no watermarks, completely free.

    Facebook Image Size Requirements

    • Posts (1.91:1): 1200×630 pixels - Recommended for link previews and shared posts
    • Cover Photo (2.63:1): 820×312 pixels - Perfect for Facebook page and profile cover photos
    • Profile Picture (1:1): 400×400 pixels - Square format for profile pictures (displays as 170×170)
    • Event Cover (16:9): 1920×1080 pixels - Wide format for event cover photos

    Why Resize Images for Facebook?

    • Better Quality: Using Facebook's recommended dimensions ensures your images display at maximum quality without compression.
    • Faster Loading: Properly sized images load faster, improving user experience and engagement.
    • Professional Appearance: Correctly sized images look more professional and polished on Facebook.
    • Algorithm Benefits: Well-optimized images may perform better in Facebook's algorithm, potentially increasing reach.

    Step-by-Step Guide

    1. Upload Your Images: Click the upload area above or drag and drop up to 50 images at once.
    2. Choose Facebook Format: Select the format that matches your content (Post, Cover Photo, Profile Picture, or Event Cover).
    3. Adjust Quality: Set quality to 90% for best results (recommended for Facebook).
    4. Resize: Click "Resize for Facebook" and wait a few seconds for processing.
    5. Download: Get all resized images in a ZIP file, ready to upload to Facebook.

    Tips for Best Facebook Results

    • Use Post format (1200×630) for shared links and general posts
    • Cover Photo format (820×312) is perfect for page headers
    • Profile Picture format (400×400) ensures crisp profile images
    • Event Cover format (1920×1080) works great for event pages
