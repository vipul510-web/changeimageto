Privacy Policy

Last updated:

ChangeImageTo ("we", "us") provides a website and a Chrome extension that help you edit images (remove background, blur, enhance, upscale, and change background color). We respect your privacy and design our products to minimize data collection.

What data we collect

  • Images you choose to process: When you use our tools, your selected image is processed either locally or sent securely to our backend to generate the output. We do not use these images for any other purpose.
  • Basic diagnostic logs: Our backend may log non-personal diagnostics such as request time, file type, and processing outcome to maintain reliability. We do not store images after processing completes.
  • No personal profile: We do not sell, rent, or share personal data. We do not build user profiles.

How we use your data

  • Provide and improve the image processing features you request.
  • Ensure service reliability and prevent abuse.

Retention

Processed images are generated on demand. We do not store your images on our servers after processing is complete. Temporary in‑memory retention may occur only for the duration necessary to return results.

Cookies and tracking

Our website may use minimal, non-identifying cookies strictly to operate the site. We do not use third‑party advertising trackers.

Third‑party services

Our backend is hosted on reputable cloud infrastructure. These providers may process data on our behalf only to deliver the service and are bound by their own security and privacy commitments.

Your choices

  • You can remove the Chrome extension at any time from your browser.
  • You can delete any generated files from your device at any time.

Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or our data practices, contact us at hello@changeimageto.com.
