Introduction

At ChangeImageTo.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our free online image editing tools.

Information We Collect

Images You Upload

We do not store your images permanently. When you upload images to our platform for processing:

  • Images are temporarily stored in memory during processing only
  • Images are automatically deleted immediately after processing
  • We do not save, backup, or archive your images
  • Images are never shared with third parties

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our services:

  • Page views and user interactions
  • Processing times and success rates
  • Browser type and device information
  • General geographic location (country level)

No Personal Information Required

Our platform does not require registration, account creation, or personal information submission. You can use all our tools anonymously.

How We Use Information

Image Processing

  • Process your images using AI algorithms
  • Return processed images to your browser
  • Immediately delete original and processed images

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize performance
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features and tools
  • Monitor system security and reliability

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated usage statistics with service providers who help us improve our platform, but this data cannot be used to identify individual users.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Automatic deletion of uploaded images after processing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to systems and data

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for essential functionality:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage statistics (Google Analytics)
  • No Advertising Cookies: We don't use advertising or tracking cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Vercel: Website hosting and CDN services
  • Cloud Run: Image processing backend

These services may have their own privacy policies, but they do not have access to your uploaded images.

Data Retention

  • Images: Deleted immediately after processing (no retention)
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous data retained for up to 26 months
  • Server Logs: Automatically purged after 30 days

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • No Data Collection: We don't collect personal data, so there's nothing to access or delete
  • Opt-out: Disable cookies or JavaScript to limit tracking
  • Contact Us: Reach out with any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't require registration or collect personal data, this is inherently protected.

International Users

Our services are available globally. By using our platform, you consent to the processing of anonymous usage data as described in this policy, regardless of your location.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
