FAQ

What is image upscaling?

Image upscaling increases the resolution and size of an image by intelligently adding pixels, making photos larger without losing quality.

Which method should I choose?

Lanczos provides the best quality but takes longer. Cubic is a good balance. Area and Nearest are faster but lower quality.
