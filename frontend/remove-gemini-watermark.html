What is Gemini watermark?

Google Gemini adds a small watermark to images generated by its AI models, typically positioned in the bottom-right corner of the image. This watermark identifies the image as being created by Google's Gemini AI. Our tool uses advanced AI inpainting technology to seamlessly remove this watermark while maintaining the original image quality and appearance.
