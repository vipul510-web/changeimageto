# ChangeImageTo.com - AI/LLM Information

## About
ChangeImageTo.com is a comprehensive suite of free online image processing tools powered by advanced AI technology. Our platform provides professional-grade image editing capabilities without requiring registration or payment.

## Core Services

### Background Processing
- **Remove Image Background**: AI-powered background removal for products and portraits
- **Change Image Background**: Replace backgrounds with solid colors (white, blue, black, yellow, orange)
- **Blur Background**: Create portrait effects by blurring backgrounds

### Color & Enhancement
- **Change Image Colors**: Adjust hue, saturation, brightness, and contrast
- **Enhance Image**: Auto-improve contrast, brightness, and sharpness
- **Image Quality Checker**: Detect blur, noise, pixelation, and exposure issues

### Format & Size Processing
- **Convert Image Format**: Convert between JPG, PNG, WebP formats
- **Upscale Image**: Increase image size up to 4x using AI
- **Bulk Image Resizer**: Resize up to 50 images simultaneously

## Technical Specifications
- **File Support**: JPG, PNG, WebP formats
- **File Size Limit**: 10MB per image
- **Processing Speed**: Most images processed in under 10 seconds
- **Privacy**: Images processed temporarily and deleted immediately
- **No Registration**: Instant access without signup required
