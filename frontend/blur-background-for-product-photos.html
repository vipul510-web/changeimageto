What's the best blur level for product photos?

For most product photos, Medium blur works best - it provides a professional look without being too dramatic. For products with very busy backgrounds, use Strong or Max blur. For minimal distraction, Light blur is ideal.

Can I use this for Amazon product photos?

Yes! Our tool is perfect for Amazon product photos. Use Medium blur to create professional product images with clean, blurred backgrounds that make your products stand out and meet Amazon's image quality requirements.

How do I blur the background of a product photo?

Upload your product photo, select a blur level (we recommend Medium for most products), and click "Blur Background". Our AI will automatically detect the product and blur only the background while keeping the product sharp.

What blur level should I use?

For product photos, start with Medium blur. For busy backgrounds or stronger effects, try Strong or Max blur. Light blur provides a subtle effect for minimal background distraction.
