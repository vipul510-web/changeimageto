How to convert background to black and white?

  1. Upload your image using the uploader above.
  2. Select the Photo Type that best matches your image (Portrait, Product, or General).
  3. Click Convert Background to Grayscale and wait a few seconds.
  4. Download your PNG—your subject stays in full color while the background becomes black and white.

Tip: For best results with portraits, select "Portrait / People". For products, use "Product / Object".

Why convert background to grayscale?

  • Portraits: Make your subject stand out with a dramatic black and white background. Perfect for professional headshots and profile photos.
  • Ecommerce: Draw attention to your products by desaturating distracting backgrounds.
  • Social Media: Create eye-catching posts with a cinematic color vs. grayscale contrast.
  • Artistic Effect: Add a creative, artistic touch to your photos with selective colorization.

How it works

Our tool uses advanced AI segmentation (U-2-Net) to automatically detect and separate the foreground from the background. The background is then converted to grayscale while preserving the full color of your subject. This creates a striking visual effect that emphasizes your main subject.
