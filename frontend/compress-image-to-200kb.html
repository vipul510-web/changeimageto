How to Compress Image to 200KB

Our free online tool makes it easy to compress images to 200KB or any target file size. Simply upload your image above, choose your target size, and click compress. The tool automatically adjusts quality to reach your target size. No signup required, no watermarks, completely free.

Why Compress to Specific File Sizes?

  • Email Attachments: Many email services limit attachment sizes (often 25MB or less). Compressing to 200KB ensures your images send successfully.
  • Website Requirements: Some websites and applications have strict file size limits. Compressing to target sizes ensures compliance.
  • Social Media: Different platforms have different size limits. Compressing to specific sizes ensures successful uploads.
  • Form Submissions: Online forms often have file size restrictions. Compressing to target sizes ensures successful submissions.
  • Storage Optimization: Compressing to specific sizes helps manage storage space efficiently.

How It Works

Common Target Sizes

  • Start with JPG format for best compression - it offers the best size reduction
