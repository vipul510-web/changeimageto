How to Convert PNG to WEBP Online

Our free online tool makes it easy to convert PNG to WEBP format. Simply upload your PNG image above and click the convert button. No signup required, no watermarks, completely free.

Why Convert PNG to WEBP?

  • Smaller File Sizes: WEBP typically produces files 25-35% smaller than PNG while maintaining similar quality. This significantly improves website load times and reduces bandwidth usage.
  • Transparency Support: Like PNG, WEBP supports transparent backgrounds, making it perfect for logos, graphics, and images that need to blend with different backgrounds.
  • Modern Web Standard: WEBP is supported by all modern browsers and is recommended by Google for optimal web performance. It's the future of web images.
  • Better Compression: WEBP uses advanced compression algorithms that provide better quality-to-size ratio than PNG, especially for photographs.

When to Use WEBP Instead of PNG

  • Website Images: WEBP is ideal for website images, reducing page load times and improving SEO rankings.
  • Modern Applications: Use WEBP for mobile apps, progressive web apps, and any application targeting modern devices.
  • E-commerce: WEBP helps reduce bandwidth costs for online stores with many product images.
  • Social Media: Many platforms now support WEBP, allowing you to upload smaller files faster.
  • Photographs: WEBP provides excellent compression for photos while maintaining transparency when needed.

Important Notes About PNG to WEBP Conversion

  • Transparency Preserved: WEBP fully supports transparency, so your transparent PNG backgrounds will be preserved.
  • Browser Support: All modern browsers support WEBP. For older browsers, you may need PNG fallbacks.
  • Quality Settings: Higher quality settings (90) provide near-lossless quality with good compression. Lower settings reduce file size further.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will WEBP preserve transparency from PNG?

Yes, WEBP fully supports transparency, so your transparent PNG backgrounds will be preserved in the WEBP file.

Is WEBP supported by all browsers?

All modern browsers support WEBP. For older browsers (IE11 and earlier), you may need to provide PNG fallbacks.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our tool is completely free with no signup required and no watermarks.

How much smaller are WEBP files compared to PNG?

WEBP files are typically 25-35% smaller than PNG files while maintaining similar visual quality.

Can I use WEBP for print?

WEBP is primarily designed for web use. For print, PNG or TIFF formats are usually better choices.
