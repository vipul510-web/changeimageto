Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, check our FAQ for quick answers to common questions:

General Questions

How long does image processing take?
Most images are processed in under 10 seconds. Processing time depends on image size and complexity.
What file formats do you support?
We support JPG, PNG, and WebP image formats. Files can be up to 10MB in size for optimal processing speed.
Are my images stored on your servers?
No. Images are processed temporarily and then deleted. We don't store or share your images with anyone.
Can I use these tools for commercial purposes?
Yes! You can use our tools for any purpose, including commercial projects, without any restrictions.

Technical Support

Why is my image processing failing?
Check that your image is under 10MB and in a supported format (JPG, PNG, WebP). Try refreshing the page and uploading again.
The background removal isn't perfect. What can I do?
Try selecting the correct category (Product, Portrait, or General) for better results. Our AI performs best with clear, high-contrast images.
Can I process multiple images at once?
Yes! Use our Bulk Image Resizer tool to process up to 50 images simultaneously and download them as a ZIP file.

Response Time

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.
