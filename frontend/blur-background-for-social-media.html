Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best blur level for social media photos?

For most social media content, Medium blur works best - it provides a professional look that's engaging without being too dramatic. For Instagram and creative content, try Strong or Max blur. For LinkedIn and professional posts, use Light to Medium blur.

Can I use this for Instagram posts?

Yes! Our tool is perfect for Instagram posts. Use Medium to Strong blur to create eye-catching content with beautiful blurred backgrounds that make your posts stand out in the Instagram feed.

How do I blur the background of a social media photo?

Upload your social media photo, select a blur level (we recommend Medium for most posts), and click "Blur Background". Our AI will automatically detect the subject and blur only the background while keeping the subject sharp.

What blur level should I use?

For social media content, start with Medium blur. For busy scenes or stronger effects, try Strong or Max blur. Light blur provides a subtle effect for minimal background distraction.

Is the background blur tool free to use?

Yes, our background blur tool is completely free to use. No registration, no watermarks, and no hidden fees.

What image formats are supported?

We support all major image formats including JPG, PNG, and other common formats. Upload your image and get instant results with a blurred background.

Can I adjust the blur intensity after processing?

You can reprocess your image with a different blur level. Simply upload the same image again and select a different blur intensity to get a new result.
