Published: January 2025 Ecommerce Photography

Amazon sellers know that professional product photos are crucial for success. One of the most important requirements is having a clean white background for your main product image. This guide will show you exactly how to achieve this using our free online tool.

Why Amazon Requires White Backgrounds

Amazon's product image requirements specify that your main product photo must have a pure white background (RGB values 255, 255, 255). This isn't just a preference—it's a requirement that affects your listing's visibility and conversion rates.

  • Better Search Visibility: Amazon's algorithm favors listings with professional product images
  • Increased Click-Through Rates: Clean white backgrounds make products stand out
  • Professional Appearance: White backgrounds look more trustworthy to customers
  • Amazon Compliance: Avoid listing rejections due to image requirements

Amazon Image Requirements Checklist

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Off-white backgrounds: Ensure pure white, not cream or gray
  • Poor product isolation: Use our AI edge detection for clean cuts
  • Low resolution: Always maintain high quality for Amazon's requirements
  • Inconsistent lighting: Maintain even lighting across your product photos

Pro Tips for Amazon Sellers

Lighting Setup

Use soft, even lighting to minimize shadows that are difficult to remove later.

Product Positioning
