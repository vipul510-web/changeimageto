Tips for Better Background Removal Results

📸 Photo Preparation

  • Use high-contrast backgrounds when possible
  • Ensure good lighting on your subject
  • Avoid complex, detailed backgrounds
  • Keep the subject centered in the frame

🎯 Subject Selection

  • Clear, well-defined edges work best
  • Avoid subjects with fine details like hair
  • Solid-colored subjects are easiest
  • Consider the subject's complexity

⚙️ Tool Settings

  • Use the highest quality settings available
  • Export in PNG format for transparency
  • Check the preview before downloading
  • Use batch processing for multiple images
