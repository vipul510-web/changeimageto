How to Remove Background From Image on Android

By: ChangeImageTo.com Team · 2025-09-23 10:59:12 UTC

Removing backgrounds from images on Android devices is easier than you might think. Here are the best methods using built-in Android features and popular apps.

Method 1: Google Photos (Built-in)

  1. Open Google Photos app on your Android device.
  2. Select the image you want to edit.
  3. Tap the "Edit" button (pencil icon).
  4. Scroll down and tap "Magic Eraser" or "Background Blur".
  5. Use your finger to highlight the background areas you want to remove.
  6. Tap "Done" and save the edited image.

Note: Magic Eraser is available on Google Pixel devices and newer Android phones with Google Photos.

Method 2: Samsung Gallery Editor

  1. Open Samsung Gallery app on your Samsung device.
  2. Select the image and tap "Edit".
  3. Look for "Object Eraser" or "Background Eraser" in the tools.
  4. Draw over the background areas you want to remove.
  5. Tap "Apply" and save your changes.

This feature is available on Samsung Galaxy devices with One UI 3.0 or later.

Method 3: Third-party Apps

  1. Background Eraser: Download from Google Play Store, upload image, use auto-detection.
  2. Remove.bg: Install the app, take or select photo, automatic background removal.
  3. PhotoRoom: Professional-grade background removal with manual editing tools.

Tips for Android Background Removal

  • Use high-resolution images (at least 1080p) for better results.
  • Ensure good lighting when taking photos for background removal.
  • Images with clear subject-background contrast work best.
  • Save results as PNG format to preserve transparency.
  • Use landscape mode for better editing precision on tablets.
