How to Blur Image in Paint

Blog

By: ChangeImageTo.com Team · 2025-12-19 22:35:00 UTC

Windows Paint is a basic image editor with limited blur capabilities. Here's what Paint offers and better alternatives for blurring images.

Blur options in Paint

Note: Traditional Windows Paint doesn't have built-in blur filters. However, Paint 3D (Windows 10/11) offers some blur effects:

  1. Open Paint 3D: Press Windows key, type "Paint 3D", and press Enter.
  2. Open your image: Menu → Open or press Ctrl+O.
  3. Click on Effects in the toolbar.
  4. Select Blur or Motion Blur from the effects panel.
  5. Adjust the blur intensity using the slider.
  6. Click Done to apply.
  7. Save your image: Menu → Save As.

Limitation: Paint 3D's blur applies to the entire image, not just the background.

Limitations of Paint

  • Traditional Paint has no blur filters.
  • Paint 3D has basic blur but applies to entire image.
  • No automatic background detection or selective blur.
  • Limited control over blur intensity and quality.
  • No advanced blur effects like Gaussian or Lens Blur.

For professional results, use our Blur Background tool instead.

Tips for using Paint 3D blur

  • Use Paint 3D (not traditional Paint) for blur effects.
  • Blur applies to the entire image - no selective background blur.
  • Adjust blur intensity gradually to avoid over-blurring.
  • Save your work frequently.
