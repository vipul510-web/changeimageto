    How to Resize Images for Instagram

    Our free online tool makes it easy to resize images for Instagram. Simply upload your images above, choose your Instagram format, and click resize. No signup required, no watermarks, completely free.

    Instagram Image Size Requirements

    • Square Posts (1:1): 1080×1080 pixels - Perfect for feed posts that display as squares
    • Portrait Posts (4:5): 1080×1350 pixels - Ideal for vertical photos that take up more screen space
    • Landscape Posts (1.91:1): 1080×566 pixels - Great for wide, horizontal images
    • Stories (9:16): 1080×1920 pixels - Full-screen vertical format for Instagram Stories
    • Reels (9:16): 1080×1920 pixels - Same as Stories, optimized for video content

    Why Resize Images for Instagram?

    • Better Quality: Using Instagram's recommended dimensions ensures your images display at maximum quality without compression artifacts.
    • Faster Upload: Properly sized images upload faster and reduce data usage, especially important for mobile users.
    • Professional Appearance: Consistent sizing creates a cohesive, professional-looking Instagram feed.
    • Algorithm Benefits: Well-optimized images may perform better in Instagram's algorithm, potentially increasing engagement.

    Step-by-Step Guide

    1. Upload Your Images: Click the upload area above or drag and drop up to 50 images at once.
    2. Choose Instagram Format: Select the format that matches your content (Square, Portrait, Landscape, Story, or Reel).
    3. Adjust Quality: Set quality to 90% for best results (recommended for Instagram).
    4. Resize: Click "Resize for Instagram" and wait a few seconds for processing.
    5. Download: Get all resized images in a ZIP file, ready to upload to Instagram.

    Tips for Best Instagram Results

    • Use Square format (1080×1080) for a clean, consistent feed appearance
    • Portrait format (1080×1350) works great for showcasing products or people
    • Landscape format (1080×566) is perfect for scenic photos and wide compositions
    • Stories and Reels use vertical format (1080×1920) for full-screen impact
    • Maintain aspect ratio to prevent distortion - our tool does this automatically
    • Use 90% quality for the best balance between file size and image quality
