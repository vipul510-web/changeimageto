When should you change a background to white?

  • Online marketplaces: Create Amazon‑style white background images for product listings, catalog images, and comparison photos.
  • Portfolio & profiles: Clean, distraction‑free white backgrounds work well for LinkedIn, resumes, and portfolio headshots.
  • Website & landing pages: White backgrounds make it easier to overlay text and keep your brand pages looking consistent.
  • Social media posts: Use white backgrounds to highlight a product or subject while keeping your feed minimal and on‑brand.
